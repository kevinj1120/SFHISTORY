Neighborhoods

San Francisco is a city small in area, but big in poplation. In a city with so many people, you can see the different neighborhoods in the city, specifically those neighborhood's unique cultures.

Neighborhoods in San Francisco have their own culture and community. Walking from one neighborhood to the next can feel like traveling to a different country. Every district is unique, and here you can explore more of how each of these areas became the way that they are today.

Chinatown

This neighboorhood is located in the northeast area of the city. Being the second largest Chinatown in the US, this is one of the best places to enjoy Chinese culture.

Downtown

This neighboornood is best know for its tall buildings and clothing stores. Always busy, walking through these streets you can see all the tall buildings, bustling crowds, and lights.
